A Thought From Stillness

John Paul Narowski

A thought from stillness
A boat gliding across the waves
A day, a week, a month may pass without existing the maze
What joy, what life, what effervescence is found inside the space
That force and will and huff and puff can simply not replace

It’s air, not fire, space, not might that guides and grows the blaze
It’s pause, not run, thought, not force that cuts right through the haze

To sit and steep in the expansive wisdom
That a single thought may pave
Makes life worth living and songs worth singing,
and courage to embrace the day

Poetry
